Coloring Basics

Warm colors — such as red, yellow, and orange; evoke warmth because they remind us of things like the sun or fire. Cool colors — such as blue, green, and purple (violet); evoke a cool feeling because they remind us of things like water or grass.

Complementary colors are opposites on the color wheel. This relationshop will produce visual tension.
Triadic colors are three colors evenly spaced on the solor wheel. One color dominates, the second supports , and the third accents.

Tint is hue with white added.
Tone is a hue with gray added.
